What is Hour 1?

Hour1 is a 2D puzzle platformer where the gameplay derives from three primary mechanics:

  1. Every surface the player interacts with is a laser. Each laser starts at an origin, and extends infinitely in a single direction unless stopped.
  2. When two lasers intersect with eachother, they both are stopped.
  3. The player can place and move a single block anywhere in a level, which will also stop lasers.
These three mechanics with the addition of multiple laser types that behave differently provide the core gameplay of Hour1, where any action by the player has the potential to radically alter the layout of a level through a series of chain reactions. It's easier to understand by watching the game in action than an explanation.

In addition to a full story mode, Hour1 will also come with a level editor for players to create and exchange their own stages.

Hour1 is planned to be released this coming Fall for PC and Linux."
